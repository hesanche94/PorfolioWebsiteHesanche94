About Me

Welcome to my GitHub profile! I'm a Data Scientist and Analyst with a passion for using data to drive insights and business decisions. For over 9 years, I led teams and managed operations in retail and healthcare, where every day required strategic decision-making, problem-solving, and adaptability. In these roles, I found myself drawn to data-driven decision-making, leveraging analytics to optimize processes, improve efficiency, and drive business success. Over time, I realized that what I truly loved was analyzing data, uncovering insights, and presenting them in ways that empowered teams at all levels to make informed choices. Despite this passion, I hesitated for years to make a career shift—until September 2023, when my father suffered a severe stroke. For seven months, I balanced the demands of a high-intensity job while managing his care. In April 2024, my dad suffered yet another major health setback, which made me realize that time is not something that I should keep taking for granted. Thus, I made the decision to fully commit to my career pivot into Data Science. Since then, I’ve immersed myself in data analytics, machine learning, and statistical modeling, working on projects that align with my deep-rooted passion for solving real-world problems with data. My goal is to bridge my operational expertise with data science, creating impactful insights that drive business and healthcare decisions.
